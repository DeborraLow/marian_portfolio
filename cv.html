Marian St. Laurent

Content Strategy & Innovation

Old stories, New technologies

Since 2004, I have consulted internationally as a specialist in cultural insight and semiotics developing immersive brand experiences for media, luxury, consumer goods and transport clients. My approach is informed by a deep understanding of the impact of technological change on storytelling trends and consumer perception.

Genre-specific studies I have conducted on developments in non fiction, original content, gaming and reality television have been syndicated for major American media clients.

Global studies I have directed have informed innovation routes and brand guidelines for news and television, digital content, UX design, transport and retail interiors.
