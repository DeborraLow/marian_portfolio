Marian St. Laurent

Content Strategy & Innovation

Old stories, New technologies

I have worked internationally as a specialist in cultural insight and semiotics developing immersive brand experiences for clients in media, luxury, consumer goods and transport sectors since 2004.

I develop creative strategies for innovation that leverage the possibilities of new technologies and changing audience values. My approach to creative strategy & innovation is informed by an intuitive and historically grounded understanding of the impact of technological change on evolving storytelling trends and consumer perception.

Creative Strategy

Power by Culture

Old stories, New technologies

I have been commissioned to conduct global studies to inform guidelines and innovation routes for transport interiors, product development, television content, flavour and fragrance innovation and packaging designs.

Genre-specific studies I have conducted to track evolving storytelling trends in television and film have been syndicated for major American media, entertainment and news clients.
