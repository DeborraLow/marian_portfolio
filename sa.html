01 / Driver in Premium Travel

After identifying trends in automotive and aviation worlds, we focused on tracking evovling storytelling trends in adjascent spaces including Arts, Design, Architecture, Materials Science, Fashion and Entertainment across markets.

Key Takeaways : All markets

  • FROM ULTRALUXE EXHIBITIONISM TOWARD A NEW VISION OF LUXURY AS A DISCREET PLEASURE OF EXCLUSIVE ACCESS
  • A HOLISTIC VIEW OF LUXURY IN WHICH RESOURCES & SLOW PRODUCTION PROCESS IS THE STORY
  • UNIQUE, PERSONAL (vs. “PERSONALIZED”) EXPERIENCES BEYOND LUXURY MEGABRANDS AS STATUS
